How do you grow rice?

Plan to sow rice seeds in the spring after the danger of frost is past. Fill a 7-gallon or larger pot with compost to an inch or two from the top of the pot. Scatter seeds densely on top of the soil, add a bit more compost, and water thoroughly, saturating the soil in the pot
